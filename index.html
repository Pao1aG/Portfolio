Welcome to my Portfolio! My name is Paola Gonzalez and I am currently a student of the Trilogy Coding Bootcamp through the University of Arizona (UArizona).

Before I took the step to delve into the exciting world of coding, I studied public health at UArizona earning both a Bachelor of Science and a Masters degree.

During my collegiate and job experiences, I served my community through many projects that have utilized my skills in organization and creativity. For example, when I worked as a Community Nutrition Educator, I designed many educational materials to teach over Zoom and directed, filmed, and edited videos for our participants. I have also managed and maintained the social media and outreach materials of different organizations.

All roads have lead me here... The time spent researching, document formatting, designing, and collaborating with people of many different backgrounds has inspired me to take on coding projects such as the ones you will see here.

I will keep this page updated with new projects. Do visit again soon!
